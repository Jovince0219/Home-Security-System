Common RTSP URL formats:
• Generic: rtsp://192.168.1.54:554/[PATH]
• Hikvision: rtsp://192.168.1.54:554/Streaming/Channels/101
• Dahua: rtsp://192.168.1.54:554/cam/realmonitor?channel=1&subtype=0
• TP-Link: rtsp://192.168.1.54:554/stream1
• V380 Pro: rtsp://192.168.1.54:554/live/ch00_0
Common HTTP/MJPEG URL formats:
• Generic MJPEG: http://192.168.1.54:554/mjpeg
• IP Webcam: http://192.168.1.54:8080/video
• DroidCam: http://192.168.1.54:4747/mjpegfeed

Setup Instructions

  1. Open V380 Pro app and go to camera settings
  2. Enable RTSP stream in network settings
  3. Note down the RTSP URL (usually: rtsp://[CAMERA_IP]:554/live/ch00_0)
  4. Find your camera's IP address in the app or router settings
  5. Enter the RTSP URL above and test connection
